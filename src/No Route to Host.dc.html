Debrief — Wrong access VLAN

Here’s what was actually broken, why the symptom looked the way it did, and how your triage scored.

The fault

Interface Gi0/1 was assigned to VLAN 20 (VOICE). Host A lives in the 10.0.10.0/24 subnet served by VLAN 10 (DATA). The access port put it in the wrong broadcast domain.

Why the symptom appeared

Host A’s ARP for 10.0.10.1 was broadcast inside VLAN 20 — the VLAN 10 SVI never heard it. Every packet to the gateway timed out, while hosts already in VLAN 10 were unaffected. Classic “only one host is down.”
